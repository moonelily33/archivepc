About

NCT JNJM Archive is a small fan-made project dedicated to organizing Jeno & Jaemin photocards, merch, dolls, and special collection items 🤍

This space was created to help fans easily browse, track, and enjoy their collections — whether for wishlists or simply admiring the archive 🌸☁️

This project is not affiliated with any official sources, and is purely made for personal and fan purposes only ⭐

Disclaimer

All credits go to the original owners 🤍 I only arranged and formatted the images into templates to make them easier to use 🌸

Please use this archive for personal purposes only — not for commercial use 🌙

Images shown may differ slightly from actual physical items due to formatting, cropping, or zoom adjustments ☁️
